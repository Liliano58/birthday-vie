A Little Backstory

You've finally turned twenty, clock ittt😂 congratulations on that, so let's start soft, shall we?
Well, at first I didn't really know what to get you or what would make you the happiest, but I knew that no matter what I got, you'd appreciate it as long as it came from the heart.

But I wanted this to feel special. I wanted it to show effort, and honestly, show you how much I really care about you.

Since I'm learning frontend development, I thought... why not make you part of this journey I'm taking? So this became both a gift for you and a reminder of how I'm trying to grow and improve myself too.

This might sound a little cringy to say, but when I started talking to you again after what happened in 100L (ghosting you and all 😭), I honestly thought you wouldn't even want to give me another chance to talk to you. So I was really glad that you did.

Honestly, after everything that happened with my ex, I thought I just needed someone to talk to, someone who could help me see things differently and make me want to socialize again. At first, I was only hoping we could fix and rebuild the friendship we had back then.

But as time went on, we kept talking more and more. You made me laugh, I genuinely enjoyed your company, and somewhere along the way I realized I was starting to develop real feelings for you.

And honestly, that scared me a little because I knew there was already someone in your life at the time.

I know you probably noticed that I didn't really like the guy 😭, but I still tried to keep myself in check and make sure I never crossed any boundaries. He probably wasn't a bad person, but sometimes the way he treated you just didn't sit right with me.

I'm not saying I'm perfect either, but deep down I just wanted you to have someone who would treat you gently, value you properly, and make you genuinely happy.

What I Admire About You

Oh and also, I want you to know something... you are a very beautiful young woman.

You are caring, you try to understand people, and I really admire how proud and confident you are in yourself. You know your worth and you don’t let people take that for granted.

There honestly aren’t a lot of people who can keep up with my energy, but somehow you do 😭 and I really love that.

Something I Realized

Honestly, one thing I really appreciate is how comfortable I feel around you. Talking to you never feels forced and somehow even the most random conversations become part of the best moments of my day.

Sometimes I catch myself wanting to tell you every little thing that happens to me, whether it's something exciting, stressful, or just completely random 😭

And I think that says a lot about how important you've become to me.

You know, ever since we got close, I've really appreciated all the time we've spent together.

The moments you made me laugh, the way you never made me feel weird for being myself, and how you always somehow manage to brighten my mood.

Being around you gives me this feeling of wanting to keep seeing you smile, and I honestly hope we can continue being there for each other, supporting one another through different moments in life.
